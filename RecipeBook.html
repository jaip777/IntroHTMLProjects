Pizza Dough

  • Time: 1 hour 30 min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 1 tsp
Dry Yeast (instant) 1 enevelope
Kosher Salt 2 tsp
Water (110 degrees F) 1 1/2 cups
Olive oil 2+ tbsp

Step 1: Combine bread flour, sugar ,yeast, in bowl. Add water and 2 tbsp of oil beat until dough forms into a ball.

Step 2: Grease a large bowl with olive oil, add dough and cover bowl with plastic wrap. Put in warm area, and let it double in size (takes 1 hour).

Step 3: Turn dough onto slightly floured surgace, divide into 2 equal pieces. Cover each with clean kitchen towel/plastic wrap. Let them rest for 10 min.

Pizza

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Tomato 2
Mushrooms 3
Cheese 2-3 handfuls
Tomato Sauce 1 can
Black Olives Handful

Step 1: Cut up all the vegetables and grate the cheese.

Step 2: Roll out the dough into a large circle. Cover with thin layer of tomato sauce, excluding edges. Cover with layer of cheese. Place vegetables as you would like. Add more cheese if necessary.

Step 3: Put on tray and put in oven set to 450 F. Watch pizza, remove when edges start to brown (10-20 min approx).

Pizza Naan

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Naan bread 8
Mushrooms 3
Cheese 2-3 handfuls
Tomato Sauce 1 can
Green Peppers 3

Step 1: Cut up all the vegetables and grate the cheese.

Step 2: Lay out the naan. Lather with thin layer of sauce, excluding edges. Put on thin layer of cheese. Broil in oven/toaster for 1 minute.

Step 3: Put on a small amount of extra cheese. Lay on vegetables. Put in oven for 3-5 min at 450 F, or toast/broil in toaster until edges brown.
